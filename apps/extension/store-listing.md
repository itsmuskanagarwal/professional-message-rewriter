## Full description

**How to Talk Corporate** rewrites your workplace messages — Slack, Gmail, Teams, Jira, LinkedIn — with the correct tone for the context. No login. No account. 10 free rewrites a day.

**How it works**

1. Type or paste your draft message in any text field on a supported platform
2. Click the floating **TW** button that appears near the text field
3. Select a tone: **Humble & Polite**, **Confident & Direct**, **Clarifying**, **Empathetic Nudge**, or **Grammar Fix Only**
4. Click **Rewrite** and copy the polished version back

**Five tone presets**

| Preset             | When to use                                                  |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Humble & Polite    | Asking for help, checking in, approaching a senior colleague |
| Confident & Direct | Pushing back on a decision, taking a firm position           |
| Clarifying         | Restating something that was misunderstood                   |
| Empathetic Nudge   | Following up on a blocked item without being pushy           |
| Grammar Fix Only   | Fixing typos and grammar while keeping your exact tone       |

**Key features**

- **No login required** — start rewriting immediately
- **10 free rewrites every 24 hours** — enough for daily use
- **Inline on any page** — floating button appears when you focus a text field
- **Platform-aware** — output is calibrated for Slack (short), email (full structure), or Jira (action-oriented)
- **Your data stays private** — message content is never stored. See our [Privacy Policy](https://professional-message-writer.vercel.app/legal/privacy).

**Supported platforms**

- Slack
- Gmail
- Microsoft Teams
- Atlassian Jira
- LinkedIn

**Privacy**

- No account, no tracking, no cookies
- Message content is processed in memory and discarded immediately
- Rate limiting uses a hashed fingerprint — raw IPs are never stored
- Powered by Anthropic Claude Haiku
